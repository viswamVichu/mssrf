Who we are

The M.S. Swaminathan Research Foundation (MSSRF) was established in 1988 with the goal of making rural lives better. The foundation has been using innovations in science and technology to come up with lateral solutions to agricultural, food and nutrition problems. Having helped 5,50,000 households and 2,50,000 fisheries across 4000 villages, we understand that there’s a lot more ground to cover and that our journey forward is only just beginning.

Our programmes make a difference. You can, too.
